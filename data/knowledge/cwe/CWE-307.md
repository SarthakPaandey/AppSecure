# CWE-307: Improper Restriction of Excessive Authentication Attempts

**Official URL:** https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/307.html

## Description

The software does not implement sufficient measures to prevent multiple failed authentication attempts within a short time frame, enabling brute force or credential stuffing.

## Mitigations

- Rate limit authentication endpoints (per IP, account, and globally).
- Progressive delays, CAPTCHA, or temporary lockouts after repeated failures.
- Monitor and alert on anomalous login patterns.
- Prefer MFA to reduce impact of password guessing.
